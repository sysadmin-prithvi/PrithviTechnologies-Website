Pricing FAQ

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all annual plans.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, ACH transfers, and wire transfers for enterprise customers.

Are there any setup fees?

No setup fees for Starter and Professional plans. Enterprise plans may include implementation services.

What if the annotations are higher than specified in the plan?

The annotations above the specified number will be charged based on an hourly rate.
